Colex is a tiny jQuery plugin which toggles a given target open and shut with a variety of possible animation and easing methods.

It is flexible enough to build each controller out of anything you like in order to fit the needs of designers and coders alike.

To see Colex in action, try the links at the upper left or the button at the lower right.
